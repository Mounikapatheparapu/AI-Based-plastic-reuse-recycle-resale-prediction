RRRPrediction — Industrial AI Project

Resale Value Model – Gradient Boosting Predicts the selling price of a used plastic item based on features like weight, condition, age, original price, and material type. Recycle Value Model – Gradient Boosting Predicts the recycling payout amount a recycler will offer, mainly driven by weight, recyclability of the plastic type, and condition. Reuse Score Model – CatBoost Predicts a reuseability score (0–100) showing how durable and reusable the plastic item is, using condition, age, and material quality.
